TRAVOYAGE-Find yourself, where your mind wants to be!

‘TRAVOYAGE – Find yourself, where your mind wants to be!’ is developed to replace the currently existing system, which helps narrow down options and hence, is more specific and time saving. The user gets to look through an array of destinations that would love to visit based on their likings.

Not only does TRAVOYAGE help in selecting the perfect destination, but also gives in a variety of websites for booking accommodation for the customers. Along with food and accommodation, the system takes care of small important things related to a customer’s travel by providing other necessary information like the best season to visit the destination, famous tourist spots and their timings. TRAVOYAGE has a facility to get tourists connected to local guides on request so that tourists get the essence of the natural or cultural value of a destination, its historical significance and other information leisure and amusement at the destination.

TRAVOYAGE also has the facility to find local cabs at a feasible rate. This has replaced the earlier system, where the customer had to look after their own local travel having no solid system to rely on.
